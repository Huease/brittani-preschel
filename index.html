ABOUT BRITTANI

Brittani Preschel is the CEO and Founder of D2 Advertising, a full-service digital marketing agency based in Ormond Beach, Florida. A Summa Cum Laude graduate of Florida State University, Brittani began her career in on-air media and radio before transitioning into digital marketing at the national agency level. She founded D2 Advertising on a single conviction: that digital strategy should be the foundation of every marketing decision, not an afterthought.

Under her leadership, D2 has grown into a Google Premier Partner with a team of fifteen specialists serving clients across automotive, legal, tourism, healthcare, and retail throughout Florida. The agency is also a Bing Accredited Partner, Facebook Marketing Partner, Google Cloud Digital Leader, and Women Techmaker Ambassador.

Brittani has been recognized as one of the Daytona Beach News-Journal's 40 Under 40 and Most Influential Women in Business, and was named to the Top 50 Women Leaders of Orlando 2026 by We Admire Women. She has spoken at Google conferences and continues to represent the next generation of digital advertising leadership.

Outside of D2 Advertising, Brittani is committed to the Volusia County business community and actively supports initiatives that advance women in leadership and entrepreneurship across Florida.
