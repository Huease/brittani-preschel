ABOUT

Brittani Preschel founded D2 Advertising with a clear thesis: digital should be the foundation, not an afterthought. A Summa Cum Laude graduate of Florida State University with a background in on-air media and national agency work, she built D2 into a Google Premier Partner with fifteen specialists driving results across automotive, legal, tourism, and retail markets throughout Florida.
