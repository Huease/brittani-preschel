Brittani Preschel founded D2 Advertising with a vision to challenge everything traditional agencies stood for. A Summa Cum Laude FSU grad with a background in on-air media, national agency work, and Google Developer community leadership, she built a team of 15 specialists driving results from automotive dealerships to law firms to tourism brands across Florida. BRITTANI PRESCHEL

Dynamic Leader, Entrepreneur, and Community Champion

Brittani Preschel is a dynamic leader, entrepreneur, CEO, community champion, digital advertising expert, and Mom. Known for her driven, diligent, and determined approach to business and her compassionate commitment to community service, Brittani has been honored by the Daytona Beach News-Journal with their prestigious 40 Under 40 award and named one of the Most Influential Women in Business.

COMMUNITY INVOLVEMENT

In her hometown of Ormond Beach, Florida, Brittani is celebrated for her dedication to the community. She serves as Chairperson of the Volusia County Community Foundation and sits on the Board of Directors for United Way of Volusia-Flagler Counties. She also advises Provision Packs, guiding the nonprofit in its fundraising and sponsorship goals.

PROFESSIONAL ACHIEVEMENTS

Brittani leads D2 Advertising, a Florida-based digital-first full-service advertising agency.
