ABOUT BRITTANI

Brittani Preschel is the CEO and Founder of D2 Advertising, a full-service digital marketing agency based in Ormond Beach, Florida. A Summa Cum Laude graduate of Florida State University, she began her career in on-air media and radio before transitioning into digital marketing at the national agency level. She founded D2 Advertising on a single conviction: that digital strategy should be the foundation of every marketing decision, not an afterthought. Under her leadership, D2 has grown into a Google Premier Partner serving clients across automotive, legal, tourism, and retail throughout Florida. She has been recognized as one of the Daytona Beach News-Journal's 40 Under 40 and Most Influential Women in Business, and was named to the Top 50 Women Leaders of Orlando 2026 by We Admire Women.

AVAILABLE TO SPEAK ON

SPEAKING TOPICS

01

DIGITAL ADVERTISING + AI IN MARKETING

Brittani breaks down how AI is reshaping the performance marketing landscape and what brands must do to stay competitive. Drawing on 20+ years of campaign data and real client results, she separates the tools that drive outcomes from those that simply generate noise. This talk is built for marketing leaders, agency teams, and business owners who need to make faster, smarter decisions with their digital budgets. Audiences leave with a clear framework for evaluating AI tools and a practical roadmap for implementation.

02

WOMEN IN LEADERSHIP + BUILDING YOUR AGENCY

Brittani built D2 Advertising from the ground up in a market that wasn't waiting for her. This talk covers the real mechanics of agency growth including client acquisition, team building, and the decisions that compound over time. She speaks candidly about what it takes to lead as a woman in a technical, male-dominated industry without softening the hard parts. This session is built for women entrepreneurs, agency owners, and anyone navigating growth in a competitive professional environment.

03

COMMUNITY IMPACT + CORPORATE RESPONSIBILITY

Brittani speaks on how businesses can align growth with meaningful investment in their local communities. Rooted in her work across Volusia County, this talk explores the practical side of corporate responsibility: how companies of all sizes can show up for the communities they operate in without treating impact as a marketing campaign. She covers what sustainable community involvement looks like, how to measure it, and why it strengthens a business from the inside out.
