About

Brittani Preschel is the founder and CEO of D2 Advertising, a digital-first full-service agency she built from the ground up in Daytona Beach, Florida. A Summa Cum Laude graduate of Florida State University, she began her career at national agencies before launching D2 and growing it into a Google Premier Partner recognized across automotive, legal, tourism, and retail markets throughout Florida. Named to the Daytona Beach News-Journal's 40 Under 40 list and honored as one of the region's Most Influential Women in Business, she brings the same standard of excellence to her community work as to her client partnerships, currently serving as Chairperson of the Volusia County Community Foundation.

Dynamic Leader, Entrepreneur, and Community Champion

Brittani Preschel is a dynamic leader, entrepreneur, CEO, community champion, digital advertising expert, and Mom. Known for her driven, diligent, and determined approach to business and her compassionate commitment to community service, Brittani has been honored by the Daytona Beach News-Journal with their prestigious 40 Under 40 award and named one of the Most Influential Women in Business.

Community Involvement

In her hometown of Ormond Beach, Florida, Brittani is celebrated for her dedication to the community. She serves as Chairperson of the Volusia County Community Foundation and sits on the Board of Directors for United Way of Volusia-Flagler Counties. She also advises Provision Packs, guiding the nonprofit in its fundraising and sponsorship goals.

Professional Achievements

Brittani leads D2 Advertising, a Florida-based digital-first full-service advertising agency. D2 was selected as a small agency Google Partner when the pilot program launched and has since earned the Google Premier Partner Badge, Bing Accredited Professional Agency status, and Facebook Marketing Partner designation.
